Your Activities!

This app takes the deciding out of your play day - weekend, travel, or just playing hooky. After providing us with your location and a few selections, you will "spin the wheel", to be given your activities for the day and evening.

Because we still want to give you some choice (if you want it), you will be provided with two day time activities and two evening ones. At this time, the day time activities will be either a hiking trail, mountain biking trail or one of each and the evening activities will be restaurants.
